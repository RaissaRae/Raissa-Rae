Crafting Code and Creativity: From Crocheting to Coding

Hello, my name is Raissa but everyone calls me called Rae. Born in Kinshasa, Democratic Republic of Congo and raised in the vibrant city of Cape Town, South Africa.
I have always been passionate about crafting, especially crocheting, and I am currently looking forward to diving into knitting and sewing once I finish this degree. However, my journey took an unexpected turn when I discovered coding.
Initially, I did not know too much about coding but once I started exploring it, I fell in love with everything about it. I enjoy learning new languages and tacking complex patterns. Some of my favourite programming languages are Java, PHP and .NET to name a few.
Ik spreek ook Nederlands op een B2-niveau. Ik heb een intensieve cursus Nederlands gevolgd, waarbij ik 12 uur per week Nederlands leerde. Dit deed ik van oktober 2021 tot juni 2022.
In addition to my love for coding and crafting, I’m always eager to learn and grow. If you’d like to connect or have any questions, feel free to reach out to me through my contact details below. I look forward to sharing more of my journey with you!

Project PHP
Project PHP is a second year, second semester course. This was a course that worked hand in hand with Web Application Development with PHP and Requirements Analysis. We were given a report which had been complied by another group and we had to make appropriate changes after speaking to the client. This time, the client was Laurien Stroobants. We had to create an application for people with memory problems, who has an informal carer who would provide support to the primary user. We had to integrate different users other than the primary user (patient), we had to have carers and admins in order for the app to be running. I was document lead so besides handling all the paperwork, I had to create sample data for the database, I had to create the profile page for each user, and the guest comment page for the guests who had could add messages for the primary user.
