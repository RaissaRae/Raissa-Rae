Internship

Business/Functional Analyst Intern

Agfa-Gevaert Group – Mortsel, Belgium

During my internship at Agfa's headquarters in Belgium, I worked as a business/functional analyst supporting with two internal projects. My role focused on gathering and analyzing business information, contributing to process improvements and also documenting current practices for future implementation.

My main responsibilities were split between two projects:

  1. Payment Term Analysis

    I supported an internal finance project by analyzing invoice payment terms within one of Agfas four systems. This involved gathering data, identifying inconsistencies and contributing to a centralized overview of existing payment agreements. The findings will serve as a blueprint to align the remaining systems across the organization.

  2. Travel & Expense Tool

    In this project, I was responsible for gathering data for particular parts of the project. I also created an "As-Is" document to describe the current expenses process in detail, this document included level 5 BPMN diagrams for both the travel procedure and the expense procedure.
